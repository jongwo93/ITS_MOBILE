3. What is the purpose of ITS?

ITS is a way to get extra practice on problems aside from homework. It tends to be relatively low-pressure and many students reported in surveys from past semesters that it was helpful.

  • Supplement homework questions with quick conceptual questions
  • Review & Test your conceptual knowledge of course material
  • Electronically grade and distribute problems

4. How are Assignments graded on ITS?

  • Each question is worth 100 pts, you need pts to obtain full score on each Assignment
  • Each Assignment consists of 40+ questions
  • ITS Assignment = Homework Assignment
  • ITS Assignment grade = *( min( Assignment_Points , )
  • In ITS under the "My Scores" link you can view your grade for each Assignment

5. What happens when I skip a question?

  • In each assignment, ITS chooses randomly one question from a bank of questions
  • When a question is skipped, another question is randomly selected from the bank, and skipped questions will eventually be selected from the bank
  • There is no penalty for skipping a question, you can skip as many questions as you like
  • ITS does allow to get back to the skipped questions, however once a question has been submitted, it will be taken out of the pool of questions

6. What is the schedule for ITS Assignments?
